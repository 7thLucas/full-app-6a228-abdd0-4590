The Journey

From silence to your first Korean

Chapters 0 & 1 — a 10-minute slice

Learning is the game

You arrive unable to read a single character. The adventure moves through the Korean curriculum — exploration teaches, dialogue quizzes, and battles assess.

By the end you can read Hangul, greet someone in Korean, and have beaten a boss doing it.

The playable path — your first ten minutes

Begin at the Letter Shrine to learn Hangul, travel to Dawn Village to practice greetings with NPCs, then face the Silence Wisp — a boss you defeat by recalling what you just learned.
