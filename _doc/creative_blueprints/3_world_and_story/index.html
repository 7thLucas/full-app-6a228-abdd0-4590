Asterra & the Hollow Sun

Kael · Ch.1 — "The Ashen Oath"

A breaking seal

Asterra is divided by eight ancient roads, each bound to a forgotten oath. Long ago, eight guardians sealed a force called The Hollow Sun beneath the continent.

The seal is breaking. Eight travelers begin separate journeys — and their stories slowly converge into one mystery.

The playable path — Kael, the Exiled Knight

Framed for murdering King Aldren, Kael wakes in a mountain chapel rescued by the healer Edrin, haunted by flashes of a masked figure, a burning throne room, and a black sun. He must escape the snowy region and survive the soldiers hunting him.
