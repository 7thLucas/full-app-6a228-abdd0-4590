Core Features

A full RPG loop in the browser

For the Player For the Creator

Explore

Side-view 2.5D exploration

Four atmospheric areas as cinematic side-view dioramas — layered parallax planes, a horizontal walkable path, smooth follow-camera, snow & fog particles, and lantern glow.

Story

Cinematic dialogue

Portrait + typewriter dialogue boxes, NPC chatter, and in-engine cutscenes with fades and camera focus.

Battle

Turn-based combat

Attack, Skills, Guard, Item, Analyze, Flee — turn-order bar, HP/SP, weakness icons, and a mini-boss duel.

Progress

Quests & growth

A tracked quest journal ("Clear Your Name"), EXP and level-ups, victory screens, and a game-over flow.

Manage

Inventory, gear & shop

Items, four equipment slots, a merchant economy with coins, and localStorage save/load at a crystal.

Foundation

Built to expand

React + TypeScript with data-driven files (characters, enemies, items, quests, maps, dialogue, skills) ready for all eight roads.
