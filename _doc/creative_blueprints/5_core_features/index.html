Core Features

A Korean lesson disguised as a JRPG

For the Learner For the Creator

Chapter 0

Learn Hangul at the Letter Shrine

An HD-2D side-view shrine teaches Hangul consonants and vowels through interactive lessons and quick recognition checks — your literacy foundation.

Chapter 1

Greet NPCs in Dawn Village

Explore a living town where NPC dialogue teaches and quizzes real Korean greetings — choosing the right Korean is how you advance the conversation.

Battle

Win by recalling Korean

Turn-based combat vs the Silence Wisp where correct answers power your attacks — Meaning Break lands hits, Fluency Combo chains them for bonus damage.

Retain

Wordbook & Memory Flame

A growing Wordbook of every letter and greeting you've learned, a daily Memory Flame streak, and XP/levels that turn one session into a daily habit.

Resume

Save & load your progress

localStorage save/load keeps chapter progress, Wordbook, streak, and XP — so a returning learner picks up exactly where they left off.

Foundation

Built to expand

React + TypeScript with a data-driven curriculum (letters, greetings/vocab, lessons, dialogue, battle question pools, chapters) ready to grow into a full course.
