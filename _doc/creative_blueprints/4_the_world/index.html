The World

A chibi pixel hero inside a living 2.5D diorama

Not a flat side-scroller and not top-down — a fixed 3/4 diorama camera over handcrafted roads, stairs and bridges that recede into depth. Arin walks all four directions (left/right across, up/down into and out of the scene) — but only along the path, never across an open plaza, so each area feels like a guided route through a real place.

4-direction exploration

Organic walkable polygons and collision — no grid, no square tiles, no single walking line.

Depth sorting & occlusion

Walk behind a lantern and be covered, or in front of a tablet and cover it. Scale shifts with depth.

Layered parallax

Far sky, deep architecture, midground props, walkable ground, and foreground framing drift at their own pace.

Cinematic lighting

Soft bloom, fog at multiple depths, contact shadows, and a vignette / depth-of-field finish.

A charming chibi pixel cast. Arin — cloak, satchel, glowing notebook — with 4-direction walk & idle animations and a feet shadow; distinct chibi NPCs who roam set paths; and Bori, a floating fox-spirit who trails with a slight delay, all depth-sorted into the scene.
