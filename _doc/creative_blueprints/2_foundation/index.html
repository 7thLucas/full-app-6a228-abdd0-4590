The Foundation

The challenge

Learning Korean stalls because the daily habit is dry. Flashcard apps feel like a worksheet, so motivation fades and beginners quit before Hangul even sticks. People want to keep showing up — but a streak counter alone isn't a reason to come back.

The slice we built

Hangul Roads turns practice into a premium HD-2D adventure where learning IS the game — quests teach Hangul, NPCs teach greetings, and battles are won by recalling Korean. A playable browser slice covers Chapter 0 (Hangul) and Chapter 1 (greetings), built on a data-driven curriculum ready to grow.
