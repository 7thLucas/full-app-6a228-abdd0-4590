The Foundation

The challenge

HD-2D turn-based fantasy RPGs are beloved — but their feel lives in polished, premium games, not the browser. A pitch deck or static mockup can't prove that atmosphere, story, and tactical combat actually come together. And any homage risks crossing into copyrighted characters, music, and UI.

The slice we built

A playable vertical slice that runs directly in the browser: an entirely original world — Asterra, the Hollow Sun, eight travelers — with real exploration, dialogue, towns, and turn-based combat. Genre homage, never a clone. One fully realized path (Kael, Chapter 1) on a data-driven foundation built to scale to all eight roads.
